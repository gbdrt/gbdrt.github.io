Guillaume Baudart

Researcher at Inria

guillaume [dot] baudart [at] inria.fr

I am a researcher at Inria in the PARKAS team at the Département d'Informatique (DI) of the École normale supérieure (ENS). My research focuses on probabilistic and reactive programming languages (language design, semantics, static analysis, compilation, inference).

From 2017 to 2020 I was a research staff member at the IBM T.J. Watson Research Center in the AI Programming Models team.
