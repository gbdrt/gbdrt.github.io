Guillaume Baudart

Research Scientist, IBM Research USA

guillaume [dot] baudart [at] ibm.com

I am a Research Scientist at IBM T.J. Watson Research Center in the AI Programming Models team. My research focuses on reactive programming languages and their applications. I am currently working on probabilistic programming, Web APIs, and semi-automatic machine learning.

I received my PhD from École normale supérieure (ENS) in Paris under the supervision of Marc Pouzet and Timothy Bourke working on reactive languages for embedded systems. My dissertation A synchronous approach to quasi-periodic systems was awarded the 2017 ACM SIGBED Paul Caspi Memorial Dissertation Award.
