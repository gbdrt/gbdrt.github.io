Guillaume Baudart

guillaume [dot] baudart [at] ibm.com

I am a Research Scientist at IBM T.J. Watson Research Center in the AI Programming Models team. My research currently focuses on every aspect of probabilistic programming: language design, implementation, compilation, semantics, inference techniques.

I received my PhD from École normale supérieure (ENS) in Paris under the supervision of Marc Pouzet and Timothy Bourke. My dissertation A synchronous approach to quasi-periodic systems was awarded the 2017 ACM SIGBED Paul Caspi Memorial Dissertation Award.
